Phase 1

VSL Preview Script - Jordan Platten

Surface: Members.affluent.co Affluent Academy 3.0 sales page · embedded above the existing curriculum bullets and buy CTA. Duration: 5-7 minutes Speaker: Jordan Platten (or pre-approved spokes) Tone: Operator-to-operator. Direct. Numbers-first. No hype. Same register as the YouTube long-form catalogue.

Why this VSL exists

The Affluent Academy 3.0 buy page is well-built. The curriculum bullets are tight. The buy CTA at ~$947 / £757 is correctly priced for the 0-10K agency operator audience. The Trustpilot 4.8 alumni wall lives on Trustpilot, not on the page.

The buyer who lands warm off a 26-minute YouTube long-form and is friction-averse converts on a 5-7 minute indoctrination video that walks them from "I watched one How To Scale Your SMMA video" into "I see exactly what I am buying for £757 and exactly what the first week looks like." The curriculum-list-only buy page does not currently carry that indoctrination on the surface.

This VSL is that surface. It does not replace the curriculum bullets. It does not replace the buy CTA. It sits above them.

Three-act script structure

Act 1 · Cold viewer indoctrination · 0:00-1:30

0:00-0:15 · Cold hook "If you landed here from a YouTube video, you've watched me say the same thing a hundred different ways: an agency is the fastest legitimate path from zero to £10K/mo a 22-year-old has in 2026. This page is the curriculum that gets you there. I'll walk you through what's inside in the next five minutes."

0:15-0:45 · Who this is for + who this is not for "This is for the operator who's either at zero and ready to sign their first three clients in the next 60 days, or stuck between £2K and £8K/mo and not sure why the next jump won't land. This is not for someone who's already past £10K/mo - the Agency Launch funnel is a better fit for that operator, and there's a link to it in the bio."

0:45-1:30 · The pattern this curriculum is built against "Every operator who's hit £10K/mo through this curriculum has followed the same five steps in the same order: pick the niche, build the offer, run the outreach, close the call, deliver the retainer. That sequence is what the eight weeks walks. The reason it works isn't because it's secret - it's because it's the same sequence I ran in my own first agency and the same sequence the named alumni on the Trustpilot wall ran in theirs."

Act 2 · Curriculum walk · 1:30-4:30

Walk the 8-week curriculum, two weeks per beat, ~45 seconds each. Each beat names: what's covered, what the operator has by the end of those two weeks, one example from a named alumnus or one Jordan-first-agency story.

  • Weeks 1-2: Mindset, health, business setup. Outcome: legal entity formed, pricing locked, niche chosen.
  • Weeks 3-4: Lead generation, outreach scripts. Outcome: first 5 sales calls booked.
  • Weeks 5-6: Sales psychology, closing, real call examples. Outcome: first signed client.
  • Weeks 7-8: Marketing science, reporting, scaling. Outcome: retainer renewed, second + third client in pipeline.

Act 3 · Buy CTA + what happens next · 4:30-6:30

4:30-5:00 · What's included in the £757 "100+ video lessons. Lifetime access. The templates, the scripts, the contract, the price ladder. The community channel. Office-hours cadence where the coaching team answers your specific questions."

5:00-5:30 · The 30-day refund "If you complete the first week and decide this is not the right curriculum for you, full refund inside 30 days. No questions, no setter call, no save offer."

5:30-6:00 · What the next 7 days looks like "Buy below. Inside 5 minutes you'll have the curriculum dashboard, your community access, and the Week 1 checklist in your inbox. The first live group call is on Tuesday."

6:00-6:30 · Close "The curriculum is below. Take the next five minutes to read the eight-week breakdown, then choose."

Production notes

  • Format: 16:9, 1080p. Spoke seated, neutral background (Affluent.co office or equivalent). One camera, one mic. No B-roll required for V1.
  • Edit: Cuts only at natural breath points. No music bed underneath the voice. Clean L-cut from speaker to title cards if needed at module beats.
  • Captions: Burned-in captions, white text, drop shadow, English. The buy page is read with sound off as often as with sound on.
  • Thumbnail: Static thumbnail of Jordan mid-sentence, gold accent overlay matching the existing Affluent palette, headline "What's Actually Inside The Academy" in the top-left third.
  • Hosting: Wistia preferred for the analytics granularity (watch-time per minute, drop-off points). Vidyard acceptable.
  • CTA placement: Buy button stays underneath the video, untouched. The VSL closing CTA verbal cue ("the curriculum is below") points the viewer at it.
  • Length discipline: Hard ceiling 7 minutes. Anything past 7 minutes loses the friction-averse buyer the VSL was built to capture in the first place.

What this VSL is NOT

  • Not a guru pitch. Jordan teaches against guru pitches every week. This is the inverse of one.
  • Not a story-arc film. No hero's journey, no origin tale. The viewer has already watched the long-form catalogue.
  • Not a Webinar. Not 60-90 minutes. Not a slide deck. 5-7 minutes, on-camera, conversational.
  • Not a paid-ad creative. This sits on the buy page, not in the ad account. Different surface, different job.

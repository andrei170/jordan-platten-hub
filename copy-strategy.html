Voice

  • Operator-to-operator, UK register. Direct, dry, numbers-first. Jordan is UK-based. Andrei is UK-based. Same time zone. Same default register. Not load-bearing in DM, mentioned once on the hub as a side note.
  • No teaching tone. Jordan teaches funnels for a living. Anything that reads as "you're missing a VSL" gets binned in the first 5 seconds. The register is "you teach this layer; here is the install version I'd ship for you."

  1. Install, not strategy. Three pieces. Less than 7 days from kickoff. Money in the bank inside the first cohort cycle.

  3. Solo + AI-leveraged + revenue-share aligned. One operator, full-stack, skin in the game. Same model class Jordan teaches his Academy students to graduate into.

Banned in this pack (Jordan-specific)

  • "Game-changer," "transform," "unlock," "supercharge," "scale to seven figures fast," "the system you need," "the funnel that will change your business"
  • "We built X for Y" applied to comp creators without verification (do not name Iman / Charlie / Sam alumni outcomes specifically; reference their funnel pages only as observable comparators)
  • "Took a fresh pass at the funnel" - banned phrase across the build. Use "studied your funnel this morning" or "made you a new plan since" instead.
  • "You're missing" / "you don't have" / "your page is weak" - any teaching-tone phrase. Replace with "the install version of this layer" / "the piece I would ship for you."
  • "We can guarantee" - no revenue guarantees. Floor / ceiling ranges only.
  • Em dashes anywhere.
  • Household-tier names (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / etc.).
  • NDA caveats explicitly written out (use "Multi-7-Figure Course Creators" only, not "NDA").
  • Memory filename references in any prospect-facing copy.
  • $ prices on the landing page hero or above the application form. Pricing range hint only in the FAQ.

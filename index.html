Why would I run this with an outside operator instead of in-house?

Because the team you have is already running the business. The Academy is fulfilling, Agency Launch is running its own application funnel, Appointwise is shipping software, and the YouTube channel is on a 936-upload cadence. Adding a 90-day VSL build, a closed-cart waitlist, a segmented book-buyer nurture, a premium Cohort tier, and a paid acquisition layer on top of an already-loaded team is how those projects either get postponed by a year or get launched at half the quality. Outside operator is faster, cleaner, and exits when production hits steady state. We hand the system to your team after the launch cycle, not before.

How much of my time does this need?

One day to film the Academy VSL. One day to film the Agency Operator Cohort positioning. Calendar holds for cohort delivery in Lever 02. The rest is build team work plus you reviewing the copy, creative, and pricing decisions before they ship. Most operators at your stage spend under 8 hours a month during the 90-day build, and under 2 hours a month after the system is in production.

Will the Agency Operator Cohort cannibalise the Academy or Agency Launch?

No. Different problems. Academy is self-paced curriculum for the agency owner building from scratch. Agency Launch is the AI-agency build programme with the "10 clients or you don't pay" guarantee. The Cohort is operating-room access for agency owners who already cleared the Academy or Agency Launch, traded the playbook into a real £8K+/mo agency, and are ready to scale past £30K/mo with cohort-grade peer feedback. Different deliverable, different audience, different buying motion. Most Cohort applicants will be Academy graduates or Agency Launch alumni moving up the ladder, not net-new buyers choosing Cohort over either of those products. The Cohort expands the ladder, it doesn't compete inside it.

How does this work alongside Agency Accession?

Accession is the $100K/mo+ in-person mastermind and currently sits behind the Agency Launch upsell ladder, no public price, no public landing page. The Cohort is a rung lower (£8K+/mo or 5+ retained clients qualifier) and is publicly visible. The Cohort sits between the Academy and Accession on the ladder, captures the operator who has outgrown the Academy curriculum but does not yet hit the Accession floor, and feeds Accession upstream as cohort members scale through £30K/mo and beyond.

What if my existing Academy already covers some of what the Cohort would?

There's overlap on some curriculum (offer setup, pricing, niche selection), but the deliverable is different. Academy is "we teach you the methodology." Cohort is "you have already proven the agency at £8K+/mo, now we operate at peer-room scale, audit your live agency, and scale offer plus retention beyond a small operator setup." Most Cohort attendees will have already passed through Academy and outgrown the curriculum. The overlap is feature, not bug. Cohort buys insider continuity for operators who don't want to start from scratch with another educator.
